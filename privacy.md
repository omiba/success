# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 7, 2026
**Application:** DBW Success Sync ("the Application")
**Publisher:** Peak Objective ("we", "us", "our")

This Privacy Policy explains how the Application handles data. The Application is an internal data-sync tool that reads weekly business metrics from QuickBooks Online and Google Drive, and writes them to a Success.co scorecard.

## 1. Data We Access

The Application reads and writes the following categories of data on your behalf:

- **QuickBooks Online (Intuit Inc.):** read-only access to Profit & Loss report figures (revenue, expense totals, account balances) for the date ranges configured.
- **Google Drive:** read-only access to specific CSV files in folders you designate, used to extract weekly operational metrics.
- **Success.co:** read access to your DataFields (measurables) and write access to post DataValues (weekly scorecard values).

The Application does not access, request, or process payment-card data, personal health information, social security numbers, or other sensitive personal data.

## 2. How We Use Data

Data is read from QuickBooks Online and Google Drive, transformed into weekly KPI values, and written to Success.co. The Application performs no other processing, analysis, or transmission of the data.

## 3. Storage and Retention

- **Authentication tokens** (QuickBooks Online refresh tokens, Success.co API keys, Google service-account credentials) are stored as encrypted secrets in GitHub Actions and in the operator's local environment file.
- **Operational data** (weekly metric values) is processed in memory during a sync run and is not retained by the Application after the run completes.
- **Logs** generated by the Application are stored in GitHub Actions for the platform's standard retention window (currently 90 days) and contain only metric labels and numeric values, not raw account data.

## 4. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data with third parties. The only data flows are between the systems explicitly configured by the operator: QuickBooks Online, Google Drive, and Success.co. We have no access to the data outside those flows.

## 5. Security

- All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
- Authentication credentials are stored as encrypted secrets, never committed to source control.
- The Application uses OAuth 2.0 for QuickBooks Online and Google service-account credentials for Google Drive.
- Refresh tokens rotate on every use and are renewed on each scheduled run.

## 6. Your Choices

You may revoke the Application's access at any time:

- **QuickBooks Online:** disconnect from your QuickBooks Online account in the QuickBooks app settings.
- **Google Drive:** revoke the service account's access in Google Cloud Console or remove the account from the relevant Drive folders.
- **Success.co:** revoke the API key in your Success.co app settings.

Revoking access immediately stops the Application from reading or writing further data.

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Application is not intended for use by children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" at the top will be updated when we do. Continued use of the Application after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
